Portfolio

  • Linear Lights Out

    In this project, I created a game called linear Lights Out where the goal is to make all of the colors match. Whenever you click on a square, the squares you clicked on and the adjacent squares will change color. The game will keep track of the amount of moves you have made and start a new game when the button is clicked.

  • Tic Tac Toe

    In this project, I created a game called Tic Tac Toe where two players try to connect their respective pieces together in a row, column, or diagonal. The game ends when either one player wins or a tie is achieved. The new game button will start a new game when clicked.
